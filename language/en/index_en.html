Mesal - Quality and Reliability in Every Drop

Our Vision

Our vision is to create sustainable and reliable water supply systems that provide access to clean water for everyone.

“Mesal” company is your reliable partner in water supply, engaged in the purchase and supply of equipment, its installation, construction, and reconstruction of water pipelines.

Based on the principles of cooperation and quality, “Mesal” unites the efforts of two countries - Turkey and Uzbekistan, to provide the most modern and efficient solutions in the field of water supply.
