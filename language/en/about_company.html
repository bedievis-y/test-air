Mesal — Quality and Reliability in Every Drop

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leader manufacturer in water & wastewater treatment technologies to ensure environmental sustainability and public health.

Mesal Water Technologies is a joint venture company located in the Yangiyul district of the Tashkent region,specializing in the manufacturing of WWTP & WTP equipment.

We design and manufacture equipment for water and wastewater treatment plants, contributing to the modernization and reconstruction of water supply and sewage infrastructure.

Mesal Company Goals

Founded on the principles of cooperation and quality, "Mesal" has united the efforts of two nations — Turkey and Uzbekistan — to deliver the most modern and effective solutions in the field of water supply.

Ensuring the Highest Quality

Providing products and services that meet international standards of quality and safety.

Customer Orientation

Striving to fully meet the needs and expectations of our customers.

Innovation and Development
